Airways Ticketing

Airways Ticketing Analysis with passenger reviews and data insights converge. From scraping flight experience reviews to performing sentiment analysis, the journey begins with scraping flight experience reviews and performing sentiment analysis to understand customer satisfaction. In the second phase, valuable data on booking patterns, flight schedules, and customer preferences takes center stage. By leveraging advanced techniques such as random forest and XGBoost classifiers, actionable predictive models are unveiled, empowering airlines to optimize pricing, anticipate passenger behavior, and enhance the overall ticketing experience.
