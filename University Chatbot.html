University Chatbot

Scraped valuable information from university websites, including FAQ pages, utilizing data augmentation techniques. By leveraging the power of Random Forest and XGBoost algorithms, the Chatbot achieves an impressive 99% accuracy rate in predicting user query categories. Taking it a step further, Learning to Rank methodology was implemented, training SVM and BiLSTM models, resulting in a remarkable 20% improvement in chatbot response recommendations
